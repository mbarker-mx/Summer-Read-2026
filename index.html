All faculty will read Fugitive Pedagogy: Carter G. Woodson and the Art of Black Teaching by Jarvis R. Givens. Faculty may then choose one additional title from the options below.

Fugitive Pedagogy

Jarvis R. Givens

Full Faculty Read

Givens examines the tradition of Black education as a form of resistance, focusing on Carter G. Woodson, the founder of Black History Month, and the teachers who carried forward a powerful tradition of “fugitive pedagogy” under Jim Crow.
